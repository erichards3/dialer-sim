Info

This website was designed to replicate a common smarphone dialer for the purposes of teaching children how to dial a number in the case of an emergency. Use this to help a child get familiar with dialing their parents' phone numbers or that of another trusted family member or friend. Or use this for whatever other purposes you desire!

No data entered on this site is collected or preserved in any way shape or form.

Also, pressing the "" button does not place a real phone call. This is just a simulator and offers no actual calling functionality.
